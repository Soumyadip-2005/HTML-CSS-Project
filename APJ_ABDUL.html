A. P. J. Abdul Kalam..

1931-2015

Missile Man Of India

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, often referred to as the "People's President" and the "Missile Man of India," was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India. A highly respected aerospace engineer, Kalam played a pivotal role in India's civilian space program and military missile development. He served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Dr. Kalam's contributions to India's space and defense capabilities were exemplary. He played a crucial role in the development of India's first satellite launch vehicle and ballistic missile systems. His leadership at the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and the Defense Research and Development Organization (DRDO) left an indelible mark on the country's technological advancements. Despite his illustrious career, Kalam remained humble and approachable, earning the affection of the masses.
